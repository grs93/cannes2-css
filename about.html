Hi - I'm Gabby - I'm a junior at the University of Miami majoring in advertising and minoring in marketing. Choosing to go Cannes study abroad was the best decision ever. I learned so much in the past week about advertising than I would've ever expected to know at 20 years old. I must also mention, midway I realized the festival was a great opportunity for networking - something I think I've gotten better at! This blog is only a small representation of how great Cannes was - I truly cannot put into words, photos, etc. of what an incredible experience this was.
